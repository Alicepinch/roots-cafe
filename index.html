About Roots

Roots cafe is an independantly run Vegetarian, Vegan and Family friendly cafe started by two friends who want to do what they can to encourage people to eat more vegetarian and vegan meals without compromising on taste. At Roots we try our best to be sustainable and are constantly working on what we can do to improve. Below are the current measures we have in place.
