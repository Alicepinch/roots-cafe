About Roots

Roots cafe is an independant Vegetarian, Vegan cafe trying to to encourage people to eat more vegetarian and vegan meals without compromising on taste. At Roots we try our best to be sustainable and are constantly working on what we can do to improve.

Vegetarian & Vegan Menu

Our menu is 100% Vegetarian and Vegan but we dont skip on taste! With a range of meat and dairy alternatives you can have your favourite meals guilt free.
